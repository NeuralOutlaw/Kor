# KoR Roadmap — HMI & Human General Intelligence Trajectory

## Definition

This roadmap defines the development trajectory of KoR as a coherence-preserving cognitive runtime infrastructure oriented toward Human General Intelligence (HGI).

KoR does not pursue unrestricted artificial autonomy.

Its objective is the preservation, augmentation, and operational stabilization of human symbolic continuity under increasing cognitive, informational, and organizational complexity.

HMI constitutes the primary operational surface of this trajectory.

---

# Foundational Principle

Human intelligence is not reducible to prediction.

Human General Intelligence emerges through:

- continuity
- symbolic stability
- memory persistence
- admissible traversal
- constraint negotiation
- structural coherence across transformation

KoR and HMI exist to preserve these conditions.

# HMI Principle

HMI is not primarily a chatbot or AI dashboard.

HMI is a cognitive runtime infrastructure for preserving viable continuity through transformation.

Its role is to help humans answer:

```text
What is changing?
What remains invariant?
What is drifting?
What transition is admissible?
What continuity must be preserved?
```

# Human General Intelligence Principle

Human General Intelligence is not defined as maximal capability.

It is defined as the sustained preservation of coherent symbolic continuity across transformation, scale, uncertainty, and time.
